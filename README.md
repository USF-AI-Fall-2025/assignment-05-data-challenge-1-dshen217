Wage columns are not normally distributed.Majority of distribution is clustered at 0, reporting no income. in histogram plot shows significant number of 0 reports. 

SEMANTICS
DISTRICT_TYPE: Identifies type of Ed district
DISTRICT_NAME: Identifies the name of the district
DISTRICT_CODE: num ID of the disctrict
ACADEMIC_YEAR: school year which data is reporting
DEMO_CATEGORY:	demographic catagory that is being measured
STUDENT_POPULATION:	sub of democatagory 
AWARD_CATEGORY:	degree earned
WAGE_YEAR1 to WAGE_YEAR4: reported average wage earned post school. year4 is target.

RELATION SHIPS BETWEEN COLUMNS

DISTRICT_TYPE, DISTRICT_NAME, and DISTRICT_CODE all identify the same educational entity at different levels (type, name, code).

EDUCATION and WAGES, higher education tends to lead to higher wages.

WAGE_YEAR1, WAGE_YEAR2, WAGE_YEAR3, and WAGE_YEAR4 are strongly related — earlier wages can infer and predict future wage growth.
